Security & Compliance Overview

We handle sensitive borrower and portfolio information for banks. Our controls are designed to align with OCC/FDIC expectations and common vendor risk frameworks.

Data Hosting & Encryption

  • Portal: Citrix ShareFile (Business/Enterprise) with org subdomain portal.clearcreditinsights.com.
  • Transport: TLS 1.2+ for all data in transit.
  • At Rest: AES-256 encryption within ShareFile-managed storage.
  • Backups: ShareFile native redundancy; critical artifacts mirrored to bank-approved storage when required.

Identity, Access, and MFA

  • Mandatory MFA for staff and client users.
  • Least-privilege access by engagement and role; per-client segmented workspaces.
  • Optional SSO/SAML for bank personnel upon request.
  • Session timeouts and password complexity policies enforced.
