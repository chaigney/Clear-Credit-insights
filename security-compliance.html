Security & Compliance

Protecting your borrowers’ confidential information is our top priority. Our processes are designed to meet the standards community and regional banks expect for secure credit analysis.

Data Security Standards

We apply layered controls across transmission, storage, access, and monitoring.

Encryption

  • In transit: TLS 1.2+ HTTPS for all file transfers and portal sessions.
  • At rest: AES‑256 encryption provided by our secure portal provider.
  • Key management: Managed by provider with strict controls and auditing.

Secure File Portal

  • All documents exchanged via an encrypted client portal (e.g., ShareFile).
  • Expiring upload links; optional client accounts with MFA.
  • Granular folder permissions and comprehensive audit logs.

Identity & Access

  • MFA: Multi‑factor authentication for staff and client portal users.
  • RBAC: Role‑based access; least‑privilege by default.
  • Session controls: Automatic timeouts and device/IP logging.

Workstation & Network

  • Full‑disk encryption on analyst devices; automatic screen lock.
  • Vendor‑managed endpoint protection and patching.
  • No borrower data stored locally beyond active work products.

Access, Retention & Deletion

Access & Permissions

  • Analysts only see client folders and files they are assigned.
  • Separation of duties for analysis and quality control whenever feasible.
  • Client banks can restrict visibility further on request.

Confidentiality & Regulatory Alignment

Confidentiality & Legal

  • NDA: Mutual NDA executed for every engagement; we can also sign your bank’s NDA.
  • Use limitations: Documents used solely for agreed analysis and deliverables.
  • Subprocessors: We disclose core providers on request; U.S. data residency preferred.

Regulatory Expectations

  • Controls and documentation designed to support third‑party risk management reviews.
  • Practices informed by FFIEC guidance and OCC bulletins on third‑party relationships.
  • Audit logs and evidence available for examiner review via our secure portal provider.
