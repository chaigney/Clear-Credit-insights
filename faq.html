Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about scope, process, security, and getting started.

Engagement & Scope

What types of loans do you underwrite?

We support C&I, CRE, construction, equipment, and working capital lines. Deliverables can include spreads, ratio analysis, UCA/global cash flow, industry benchmarking, and a credit memo in your template.

Do you provide risk rating recommendations?

Yes. We can provide a risk rating recommendation with numeric and narrative rationale aligned to your policy and examiner expectations.

Can you help with portfolio reviews?

Yes. We perform independent risk rating validation, concentration analysis, stress testing, and provide a portfolio summary with recommendations.

Do you work with examiners?

We prepare examiner‑ready documentation and can respond to reasonable information requests through your team. We do not communicate directly with regulators unless engaged to do so by the bank.

Process & Timelines

How fast can you turn around a request?

Typical engagements range from a few business days for a standard credit memo to a few weeks for a larger review. We’ll commit to a timeline during scoping, and rush requests can be accommodated when capacity allows.

How do we send and receive documents?

Through our encrypted ShareFile portal. We can provide expiring upload links or client accounts with MFA. All deliverables are returned through the same portal.

Can you work in our templates?

Yes. We can produce memos and worksheets in your templates or provide ours if you prefer.

Security & Compliance

How do you protect confidential data?

We use an encrypted client portal (ShareFile), MFA, role‑based access, and device encryption. See our Security & Compliance page for details.

Do you store borrower documents locally?

No. Documents are kept in the secure portal, and local copies are minimized to active work only and removed per policy.

What insurance do you carry?

Professional Liability (E&O), Cyber Liability, and General Liability. Certificates of Insurance are available upon request; see our Vendor Due Diligence page.

Working Together

Do you offer one‑off projects or ongoing support?

Both. We can support a single underwriting request, a defined portfolio review, or recurring capacity support.

Do you outsource work overseas?

No. All analysis is performed by our team and reviewed for quality before delivery.

How do we start?

Send us a brief scope via the contact form, and we’ll set up a quick call to confirm timeline, deliverables, and portal access.
